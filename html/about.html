About Me

A little about me

I’m Miguelle,
A self-taught web developer with three years of coding experience. By day, I work as a Manufacturing Engineer at Boeing. But my dream is to eventually transition into full-time web development, a path I fell in love with during my second internship.

It was during that internship, while working alongside a Human Factors Engineer, that I was introduced to the world of user experience. I started taking courses, learning how websites are built, and realized that everything we interact with online is created by someone. That someone could be me.

Ever since, I’ve been passionate about coding websites that feel meaningful. Websites that help people and make their business feel real and visible.

What I Do

My Approach

I build everything by hand using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No drag and drop, no templates. That means the sites I create can’t be edited on the back end unless you know how to code.

My job is to be the “wizard behind the curtain,” turning your ideas into a site that looks and feels exactly right, so there’s no need for you to worry about the tech side at all.

How I Work

I like to keep things simple, personal, and collaborative, so here's how it works:

1

Discovery Call and Questionnaire

We will kick things off with a quick discovery call so I can learn about your business and you can ask anything you want. If you are ready to move forward, I will send a short 10 question questionnaire to make sure we are aligned on what you need.

2

Deposit, Content, and Getting Started

Once you say yes, the design portion is paid in full to get started, and I collect a coding deposit to lock in your spot. For most projects it is 50 percent upfront and the rest is due when your site is ready to launch. Send over any content you have, and if you are still gathering things we can use placeholders for now.

3

Build, Domain Setup, and Launch

After the design is done, I start coding and help you get your domain and hosting set up along the way. When everything is finished, I launch your site and you are ready to share it with the world!
